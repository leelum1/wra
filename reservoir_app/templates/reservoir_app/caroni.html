Caroni-Arena Reservoir

The Caroni-Arena Reservoir is the largest reservoir in the country. It was built by damming the Arena River. The areas served include Port-of-Spain.

The Caroni-Arena reservoir is unique in that water from the reservor is not directly piped to the water treatment plant. Instead, the Caroni Water Treatment plant pulls water from the Caroni river. At times of low flow in the river, which is typical in the dry season, water is released from the reservoir into the Caroni River over the Tumpuna weir, which thens flows down the natural course fo the river to the treatment plant. The opposite happens when there is a surplus of water in the river. Water is pumped from the river to the reservoir if the reservoir is not already spilling.
