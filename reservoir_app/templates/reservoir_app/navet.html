Navet Reservoir

The Navet reservoir serves the majority of South Trinidad, including San Fernando, Moruga, and Rio Claro. The reservoir was commissioned in 1962

The Lower Navet Dam supplements the supply from the main reservoir.

Climatic stuff

The spillway design at the Navet reservoir is a labyrinth spilway, allowing for more discharge at low head.
